\begin{exercise}

If $L(t) = 3t + 5$, then its graph is a line. What is the slope of this line? \\
slope = $\answer{3}$

\end{exercise}




\begin{exercise}

Let $L$ be a line on the $kp$-axes.  Suppose $L$ has slope $4$ and $k$-intercept $(0, -3)$.  \\
This line represents a linear function $D$.  \\
Give a formula for $D$.
$D(k) = \answer{4k - 3}$ 

\end{exercise}
